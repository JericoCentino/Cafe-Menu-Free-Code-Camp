Pindtados Kasadyaan Festival

The Pintados-Kasadyaan Festival is a massive cultural and religious celebration held every June 29th in Tacloban City, Leyte. Often called the "Festival of Festivals," it is actually a beautiful, energetic blend of two distinct celebrations that were merged to showcase the collective pride, history, and faith of the entire province.

The Main Event: Festival Highlights & Showdowns

  • The "Pagrayhak" Grand Street Parade
  • The Ritual Dance Presentation
  • Sangyaw Parade of Lights
  • Fireworks Display

Festival Headliners: VIPs and Special Guests

  • National & Local Government Officials (VIP)
  • Celebrities, Beauty Queens, and Performers
  • Cultural Diplomats, Judges, and Vloggers
